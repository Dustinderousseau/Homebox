Questions
Things parents ask us.
How does campus delivery work?
We deliver directly to your student's dorm on the first Friday of every month. No shipping label required — we walk it there ourselves.
What if my student isn't in their room?
We coordinate with the dorm front desk for secure package pickup. Your student will get a text letting them know their box has arrived.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes, completely. Cancel before the 25th of any month and you won't be charged for the following box. No questions asked.
What if my student has allergies or dietary needs?
Just let us know when you sign up. We'll make note of any dietary restrictions and make sure every box is safe for your student.
How does the personal note work?
Each month we email you 3–4 simple prompts. Your answers become a printed card inside the box — in your words, not ours.
Which campus do you deliver to?
We're currently delivering on one campus and expanding. Enter your email and we'll let you know when we reach your student's school.
